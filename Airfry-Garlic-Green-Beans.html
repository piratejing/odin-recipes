Airfry Garlic Green Beans

Description:

The airfryer has become so versatile in making all sorts of dishes. This green bean recipe is no exception. Fast and easy. Plus cleanup is a breeze! It is made with only 5 ingredients and packs plenty of flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag green beans
  • 6 minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. airfry green beans at 400F for 10 mins
  2. mix in garlic and sauces
  3. airfry for another 10 mins
