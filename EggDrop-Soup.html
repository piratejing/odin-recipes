Eggdrop Soup

Description:

Classic chinese comfort food! Easy to make in under 15 minutes. This delicious soup is a staple among all chinese families.

Ingredients:

  • 4 cup chicken Stock
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp green onion for topping
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp white pepper

Instructions:

  1. whisk together stock, cornstarch, salt, sugar, sesame oil, white pepper
  2. add stock to stock pot and let simmer and thicken
  3. in a separate bowl whisk eggs
  4. stir stock in circular motion while slowly adding eggs
  5. serve and enjoy!
